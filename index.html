The EPS system effectively integrates economy and logistical efficiency in construction, ensuring high levels of quality and exceptional environmental comfort. This system is notable for its resistance to earthquakes, its performance in terms of thermal and acoustic insulation, as well as the durability, comfort, and energy savings that the buildings provide. Commitment to Workplace Safety!

At Fortress Builders, the safety of our employees is a fundamental priority. We take pride in holding the OSHA 10 certification, which ensures that our team is trained in the best safety practices in the workplace. This training not only equips our workers with the necessary tools to identify and prevent hazards but also fosters a culture of safety that permeates our entire organization.

We believe that a safe work environment not only protects our employees but also enhances productivity and the quality of our work. We are committed to continuously raising our safety standards and ensuring that every member of our team returns home healthy and safe at the end of each day.
